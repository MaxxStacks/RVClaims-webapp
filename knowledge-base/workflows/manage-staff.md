# Managing Your Dealership Staff

As a Dealer Owner, you can invite team members to help manage your dealership's operations in DealerSuite360. Staff members have operational access but cannot access billing or account administration.

## Inviting a Staff Member

1. Navigate to **Settings** → **Staff Management** (or **My Staff** in the sidebar)
2. Click **Invite Staff Member**
3. Enter their email address, first name, last name, and job title
4. Click **Send Invitation**

The invited person receives an email with a link to create their account. Once they accept and create their account, they appear in your staff list as **Active**.

**Note:** If they don't receive the email, check their spam folder. You can resend the invitation from the staff list.

## Staff Roles Explained

### Dealer Owner
- Full access to everything: claims, units, clients, billing, staff management, settings
- Can invite and remove staff
- Manages subscription and payment methods
- Sees all financial information including invoices and wallet balance

### Dealer Staff
Operational access only. Staff can:
- Create and manage claims
- Add and edit units
- Upload photos
- View claim statuses and history
- Access the client portal management tools
- Use DS360 Assist for support

Staff **cannot**:
- Access billing, invoices, or subscription settings
- View wallet balance or transaction history
- Invite or remove other staff members
- Change dealership settings
- See account manager contact information

## Removing a Staff Member

1. Go to **Staff Management**
2. Click the staff member's name
3. Click **Deactivate** or **Remove**
4. Confirm the action

Removed staff immediately lose access. Their historical activity (claims they created, photos they uploaded) remains in your account.

## Best Practices

- **Separate logins for each person** — Never share login credentials. Every action is logged with the user who performed it.
- **Use staff accounts for technicians** — Technicians can upload repair photos and update claim notes without accessing billing.
- **Review staff regularly** — When employees leave, deactivate their accounts promptly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can staff see my billing information?** No. Billing, invoices, wallet balance, and subscription settings are visible to Dealer Owner only.

**Can I have multiple Dealer Owner accounts?** No — each dealership has one Owner account. Additional users are Staff.

**What if a staff member forgets their password?** They use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You cannot reset their password for them.
