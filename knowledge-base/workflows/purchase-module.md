# Adding Modules to Your Subscription

DealerSuite360 is a modular platform. Your base subscription includes the Claims Processing module. Additional service modules can be activated as your business needs them — each adds new capabilities and a corresponding fee.

## Available Modules

### Module 1: Claims Processing (Included in all plans)
Your core subscription. Covers A-Z warranty claims management for DAF, PDI, Warranty, Extended Warranty, and Insurance claim types across all supported manufacturers.

### Module 2: Financing Services
Access to our lender network for customer financing applications. Includes approval optimization, real-time status tracking, and bilingual compliance documentation.

### Module 3: F&I Outsourcing
Complete Finance & Insurance management: loan origination, GAP insurance, extended warranty upsell, premium product portfolio, compliance documentation, and revenue reporting.

### Module 4: Parts & Accessories Management
Inventory optimization, installation service tracking, customer upgrade programs, and proactive accessory sales tools.

### Module 5: Digital Marketing & Lead Generation
SEO campaigns, PPC management, social media, lead generation, and CRM integration to grow your dealership's customer base.

### Module 6: Marketplace & Auctions
List inventory on the dealer network marketplace and participate in live wholesale auctions to buy and sell units.

## How to Activate a Module

1. Navigate to **Settings** → **Subscription & Modules** (Dealer Owner only)
2. Browse available modules and click **Activate** on the one you want
3. Review the pricing and billing terms
4. Confirm with your payment method on file

Modules activate immediately. Your monthly invoice will reflect the new fees on your next billing cycle or in a prorated amount depending on when you activate.

## Billing for Modules

Module fees are charged monthly alongside your base subscription fee. All charges appear on a single unified monthly statement. You can view your module fees in **Billing** → **Invoices**.

## Deactivating a Module

You can deactivate a module at any time from the same Subscription & Modules page. Deactivation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. Data associated with the module (e.g., financing applications, parts orders) is retained for 12 months.

## Questions About Pricing?

Contact your Account Manager directly or reach out via **DS360 Assist** → **Email Account Manager**. Module pricing is negotiated based on your dealership's volume and specific needs.
