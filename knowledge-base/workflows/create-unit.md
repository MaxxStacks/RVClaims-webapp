# How to Create a New Unit

Adding a unit to your inventory is the first step before filing any claim. Every claim is tied to a specific unit identified by its VIN.

## Step-by-Step

### 1. Navigate to Units
From your dashboard sidebar, click **Units** (or **My Units**). Then click the **Add Unit** button in the top right.

### 2. Enter the VIN
The Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) is 17 characters and is found on the unit's dashboard, door jamb sticker, or title document. Enter it carefully — the VIN is the primary tracking key for all claims on this unit.

**Auto-population:** After you enter a valid VIN and press Tab, the system will attempt to auto-populate the year, manufacturer, and model. Review these fields — if the data looks incorrect, you can override them manually.

### 3. Fill in Unit Details

**Required fields:**
- **VIN** — 17 characters, no spaces
- **Manufacturer** — e.g., Jayco, Forest River, Heartland
- **Model** — e.g., Eagle 355MBQS
- **Year** — model year of the unit
- **RV Type** — Travel Trailer, Fifth Wheel, Class A, Class C, Van Camper, etc.

**Optional but recommended:**
- **Stock Number** — your internal tracking number
- **Colour** — exterior colour
- **Purchase Date** — when your dealership acquired the unit
- **Warranty Expiry** — manufacturer warranty end date
- **Customer Name** — if the unit has been sold or assigned to a client

### 4. Upload Unit Photos
While not required to save the unit, photos help with context when filing claims. Unit-level photos are different from claim photos — these are general shots of the RV (exterior, interior overview).

To add photos: use the **Photos** tab on the unit detail page after saving.

### 5. Save the Unit
Click **Save Unit** or **Create Unit**. The unit will appear in your inventory list and is immediately available for claim creation.

## What Happens After Saving

- The unit appears in your **Units** list with status **Active**
- You can now create claims against this unit
- The unit is visible to operator staff when processing your claims
- If you assigned a client, they can view the unit in their Customer Portal

## Common Issues

**"VIN not found"** — Try entering the VIN manually and select the manufacturer from the dropdown. Not all VINs are in our lookup database.

**Wrong model auto-populated** — Override it manually. The auto-populate is a convenience feature, not a definitive source.

**Duplicate VIN error** — This VIN already exists in your account. Search for it in your unit list rather than creating a duplicate.
