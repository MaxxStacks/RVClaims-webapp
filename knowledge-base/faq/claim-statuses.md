# Understanding Claim Statuses

Each claim in DealerSuite360 moves through a series of statuses as it progresses from submission to payment. Here's what each status means and what to expect.

## Status Definitions

### Draft
You've started filling out the claim but have not submitted it yet. Claims in Draft are only visible to your dealership — the operator team has not reviewed them. You can edit all fields while a claim is in Draft.

### Submitted
You've submitted the claim to the operator team for review. You can no longer edit the claim details. The operator will review photos, FRC codes, and descriptions before submitting to the manufacturer.

### Under Review
The operator team is actively reviewing your claim. They may reach out via the claim notes or via Assist if additional information is needed.

### Preauth Requested
The operator has submitted a pre-authorization request to the manufacturer. You'll see the manufacturer-assigned Preauth # logged in the claim once it's received.

### Approved / Partially Approved
The manufacturer has reviewed the claim:
- **Approved** — All FRC lines have been approved as submitted.
- **Partially Approved** — Some lines were approved, others denied. Check the individual line statuses for details.

At this stage, you can begin or continue the repair work.

### Denied
The manufacturer has denied the entire claim. This may be due to inadequate documentation, incorrect FRC codes, or coverage exclusions. See "What to Do When a Claim is Denied" for next steps.

### Parts Ordered
Parts required for the approved repair have been ordered. You'll see the part name, number, and expected delivery date in the claim details.

### In Repair
The repair work is actively underway. Upload repair completion photos when done.

### Invoiced
The repair is complete and an invoice has been generated. Check your Billing section for payment details.

### Payment Received
The manufacturer has paid the claim. Payment is reconciled and your account is updated.

### Closed
The claim is fully resolved and archived. All payments have been received and reconciled.

## Per-Line Statuses

Each FRC line within a claim can have its own status. A claim may be "Partially Approved" while individual lines are "Approved," "Denied," or "Pending." Always check line-level detail for accurate information.

## Getting Help

If a claim has been in any status for longer than expected, use DS360 Assist or contact your Account Manager. Our operators track all active claims and will follow up with manufacturers on your behalf.
