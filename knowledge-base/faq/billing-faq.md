# Billing & Subscription FAQ

**Note:** Billing information is only accessible to the Dealer Owner role. Staff members cannot view billing, invoices, or payment details.

## How Does Billing Work?

DealerSuite360 offers two subscription plans:

### Plan A: Monthly Subscription
A fixed monthly fee (based on your negotiated rate) charged automatically via Stripe at the start of each billing cycle. You also pay per-claim processing fees when claims are submitted on your behalf. Invoices are generated monthly.

### Plan B: Pre-Funded Wallet
No monthly subscription fee. Instead, you pre-load a balance into your DS360 Wallet using Stripe (credit card) or Interac e-Transfer. Fees are automatically deducted from your wallet balance each time a claim is processed. When your balance runs low, you receive an alert to top up.

**Which plan is better?** Plan B is typically better for dealerships with variable claim volume. Plan A suits dealerships with consistent monthly claim volume who prefer predictable billing.

## What Payment Methods Are Accepted?

- **Credit Card via Stripe** — Visa, Mastercard, AMEX. Card is stored securely and charged automatically.
- **Interac e-Transfer** — Available for Canadian dealers. Send to our designated e-Transfer email. Manual reconciliation applies (1–2 business day processing time).

## Where Can I See My Invoices?

Go to **Billing** → **Invoices** in your dealer dashboard. Each invoice shows:
- The billing period
- Individual claim processing fees
- Any module fees
- Total charged and payment method

You can download any invoice as a PDF for your accounting records.

## How Are Claim Processing Fees Calculated?

Fees are calculated per claim based on the total approved value and your plan's fee schedule. Your exact fee structure is outlined in your dealership agreement. Contact your Account Manager if you need a copy of your fee schedule.

## What Happens If My Wallet Balance Runs Out?

If your wallet balance is insufficient to cover a processing fee:
1. You receive a low balance notification
2. Claims in process may be paused until your balance is topped up
3. Top up immediately via **Billing** → **Top Up Wallet**

You can enable **Auto Top-Up** to automatically recharge your wallet when the balance falls below a threshold you define.

## Can I Cancel My Subscription?

Yes. Contact your Account Manager or submit a cancellation request through the platform. Your access continues through the end of your current billing period. Historical data (claims, units, clients) is retained for 12 months after cancellation.

## Tax and GST/HST

Canadian dealers are subject to GST/HST as applicable by province. US dealers are billed in USD without Canadian taxes. Tax details appear on each invoice.
