# Common Issues and Solutions

Quick answers to the most frequently reported issues in DealerSuite360.

## "I can't see the billing page"

**Cause:** Your account is a Dealer Staff account, not a Dealer Owner account. Billing, invoices, subscription, and wallet information are only accessible to the Dealer Owner role.

**Solution:** Ask your Dealer Owner to log in and handle billing tasks. If you believe you should have owner access, contact your Dealer Owner to check your account settings.

## "My claim was denied"

**What to do:**
1. Go to the denied claim and review the line-by-line denial reasons logged by your operator
2. Check the FRC codes — the manufacturer may have denied because the code doesn't match the described repair
3. Review your photos — insufficient or poor-quality photos are a leading cause of denials
4. Contact your Account Manager via DS360 Assist → "Email Account Manager" to discuss options

**Appeal process:** Some manufacturers allow resubmission with additional documentation. Your operator team will advise on whether an appeal is possible.

## "I can't submit a claim"

**Cause:** One or more required fields are incomplete. The most common missing items:
- Photos are missing on one or more FRC lines (required per line)
- A 3C description is incomplete (must have Complaint, Cause, and Correct)
- FRC codes have not been confirmed

**Solution:** Review each claim line. Lines with issues will show a warning indicator. Address all warnings before attempting to submit.

## "A staff member didn't receive their invitation email"

**Steps to resolve:**
1. Ask them to check their spam/junk folder — invitation emails occasionally land there
2. Verify the email address is spelled correctly in their staff profile
3. Go to **Staff Management** → click their name → **Resend Invitation**
4. If still not received after 10 minutes, try a different email address

## "VIN not found" or "VIN invalid"

**VIN not in our database:** Enter the unit details manually. Our VIN lookup database covers most North American RVs but is not exhaustive. Select the manufacturer from the dropdown and fill in the year, model, and type manually.

**VIN already exists:** This VIN is already in your account. Search for it in your Units list. If you don't see it, it may have been previously archived — contact support.

**VIN format error:** VINs are exactly 17 characters. Common mistakes: the letter O confused with the number 0 (VINs never contain O, I, or Q), or extra spaces.

## "My photos won't upload"

**File too large:** Maximum 10MB per photo. Use your phone's camera at normal settings (not maximum RAW resolution). Most phone cameras produce JPGs under 5MB.

**Wrong file format:** Only JPG, PNG, and HEIC are accepted. Screenshots (PNG) are acceptable but RAW files from DSLRs are not.

**Slow internet:** Try uploading on a stable WiFi connection. Photos upload one at a time — don't close the browser during upload.

## "I can't add a new staff member"

**Cause:** You may have reached your staff account limit on your current plan, or your subscription status may need updating.

**Solution:** Check your plan limits under **Billing** → **Subscription**. If you need additional staff seats, contact your Account Manager.

## "The customer portal link isn't working"

Send the client directly to their portal login URL. They should have received an email invitation — ask them to check spam or use **Resend Invitation** from their client profile.

## I can't find my claim on the list

Claims are searchable by unit VIN, claim number, or client name. Use the search bar at the top of the Claims page. If a claim was archived or closed, toggle the filter to show "All" statuses, not just active ones.
