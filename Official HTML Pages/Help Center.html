Frequently Asked Questions

The top 5 questions our support team receives.

How do I reset my password?
+
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox. If you don't receive it within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support.
How do I add a staff member?
+
Go to Settings → Staff Management in your dealer dashboard. Click "Invite Staff," enter their email and select their role (Dealer Owner or Dealer Staff). They'll receive an invitation email to create their account.
Why was my claim denied?
+
Claim denials are issued by the manufacturer, not DS360. Common reasons include incomplete documentation, incorrect FRC codes, or warranty expiration. Check our Claim Guides for denial prevention strategies.
How do I change my subscription plan?
+
Contact your account manager or email support to discuss plan changes. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.
Can I integrate DS360 with my existing DMS?
+
DS360 is designed as a standalone platform, but API access is available for enterprise dealers who need to sync data with existing systems. Visit the API Access page for details.
Is my data secure?
+
Yes. DS360 uses bank-grade encryption, SOC 2-compliant hosting on Railway, and PIPEDA-compliant data handling. Each dealer's data is isolated in a multi-tenant architecture.
