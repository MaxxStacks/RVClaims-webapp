Dealer App

Scan. Capture. Push to Claim. From the Lot.

The Dealer App is built for the service bay and the lot — not the desk. Your technicians and service staff use it to scan unit tags, capture claim photos, read documents, and push batches of work directly into the DS360 processing queue. No walking back to the computer. No manual data entry. No lost photos.

AI Document Scanner

Point the camera at a warranty certificate, invoice, or inspection report — AI reads the document and auto-populates platform fields

Unit Tag Scanner

Scan the physical VIN plate or spec tag on any unit — AI reads VIN, weight, brand, model and auto-populates the unit dashboard

Photo Capture for Claims

Take claim photos directly in the app — they are tagged to the VIN and organized in the correct photo category automatically

Push to Claim

Batch multiple items — photos, documents, notes — and push them into the DS360 Processing Queue with one tap. Operator is notified instantly.

Real-Time Notifications

Claim status changes, parts delivery updates, and deadline alerts pushed directly to the app

Offline Mode

Works in areas with poor connectivity — data syncs automatically when the connection returns

The Mobile Workflow

Lot to Processing Queue in 5 Steps

This is the workflow your technician runs from the lot — no computer required.

1

Scan Tag

Point camera at VIN plate. AI reads and creates or finds the unit.

2

Create Unit

If new, AI auto-populates specs from the tag scan. If existing, unit dashboard opens.

3

Take Photos

Capture damage, defect, or inspection photos. Auto-tagged to VIN and category.

4

Scan Docs

Scan warranty cert or report. AI extracts data and files it in the unit record.

5

Push to Claim

One tap. Everything enters the Processing Queue. Operator notified.

Client App — White-Label

Your Branded App in Your Client's Pocket

The Client App is the mobile version of the Client Portal — white-labeled with your dealership's logo and colors. Your clients download it and see your brand, not DS360. It gives them mobile access to everything the web portal offers, plus native features like camera-based issue submission and push notifications for claim updates.

Your Brand on Their Phone

Your logo, your colors — the app looks like you built it. DS360 is invisible.

File Claims via Camera

Client photographs an issue and submits through the app — photo, description, and VIN are packaged and sent to your dealership and DS360

Purchase Protection Products

Clients can browse and purchase F&I protection products directly through the app — AI F&I Presenter accessible on mobile

Request On-Site Service

One tap to request on-site repair — client submits photos, location, and issue description. Nearest DS360 partner dealer is dispatched.

Vehicle Specs & Warranty Status

Full unit details, warranty dates, coverage type, and claim history — accessible anytime from the client's phone

Technology Approach

PWA First. React Native Next.

The DS360 Mobile App launches as a Progressive Web App (PWA) — installable directly from the browser with no app store required. This means faster deployment, instant updates, and cross-platform compatibility on both iOS and Android from day one.

React Native development is planned for a follow-up release, bringing the app to the Apple App Store and Google Play for native distribution. PWA users will transition seamlessly — same account, same data, enhanced native features.
