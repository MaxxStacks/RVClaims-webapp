1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember your preferences, keep you logged in, and understand how you interact with the platform. DS360 uses cookies on both our marketing website and the platform portals.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for platform functionality. These handle authentication, session management, security tokens, and portal routing. Essential cookies cannot be disabled without breaking platform functionality.

  • Session cookies for user authentication (JWT)
  • Security cookies for CSRF protection
  • Portal routing cookies for multi-tenant isolation
  • Language preference cookies (EN/FR)

Analytics Cookies

Used to understand how visitors interact with our website and platform. This data helps us improve the user experience and identify areas for optimization.

  • Google Analytics — page views, session duration, navigation patterns
  • Platform usage analytics — feature adoption, workflow completion rates

Marketing Cookies

Used for targeted advertising and remarketing campaigns through Google Ads, Outbrain, Taboola, and AdRoll. These cookies track browsing behavior to serve relevant ads across the web. Marketing cookies can be disabled without affecting platform functionality.

  • Google Ads — paid search and display remarketing
  • Outbrain — content discovery and native advertising
  • Taboola — content recommendation and audience targeting
  • AdRoll — retargeting and display advertising

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. DS360 does not control these cookies. Each third-party service has its own cookie and privacy policy:

  • Google: Analytics and advertising cookies
  • Stripe: Payment processing security cookies
  • Outbrain: Content discovery tracking cookies
  • Taboola: Content recommendation tracking cookies
  • AdRoll: Retargeting and display advertising cookies

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies, set preferences for specific sites, and browse in private/incognito mode. Note that blocking essential cookies will prevent the DS360 platform from functioning properly.

To opt out of marketing and advertising cookies specifically, you can visit the opt-out pages provided by each advertising platform, or use industry opt-out tools such as the Digital Advertising Alliance’s opt-out page.

5. Updates to This Policy

DS360 may update this Cookie Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. The "Last Updated" date at the top indicates the most recent revision. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
