The Components That Make an RV an RV — Unprotected

Extended Warranty covers drivetrain, electrical, and plumbing. But what about the generator that powers the campsite? The solar system that keeps the batteries charged off-grid? The leveling system that keeps the unit stable? The slide-outs that double the living space?

These are the components your clients use the most — and the ones that cost the most to repair when they fail. A generator replacement runs $3,000 to $5,000. A leveling system repair is $1,500 to $3,000. A slide-out motor that Extended Warranty does not cover is $2,500. Specialty Protection covers all of it.
