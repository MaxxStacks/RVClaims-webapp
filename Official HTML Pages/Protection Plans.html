The Full Picture

Extended Warranty and GAP Cover the Big Risks. Protection Plans Cover Everything Else.

Extended Warranty handles major mechanical systems. GAP handles the financial exposure on a total loss. But what about the surfaces, the tires, the trip, and the RV-specific components that make the unit livable? That is where Protection Plans come in.

These four products fill every remaining gap — and when bundled with Extended Warranty and GAP, they give your client complete, head-to-toe coverage on their unit. For your dealership, attaching 5 or 6 products per unit unlocks the highest cashback tier in the DS360 incentive program.

Key Details

What Dealers Need to Know Before Selling

Co-Branded with Your Dealership

Every protection product is issued under your dealer name — certificates, client communications, and claims experience all carry your branding. DS360 is invisible to the client.

Available on New and Used Units

All four protection products can be sold on both new and pre-owned RVs. A baseline condition assessment may be required at the time of sale for certain products on used units.

Claims Through the DS360 Platform

All protection claims are processed through the same A-Z workflow used for warranty and Extended Warranty claims. The client submits through the Client Portal or contacts your dealership. DS360 handles approval, fulfillment, and payout.

Wholesale Pricing — You Set the Retail

Your dealership pays DS360 the wholesale cost for each product. You set your own retail price to the client. The difference is your margin. Volume-based cashback incentives apply on top of that margin.
