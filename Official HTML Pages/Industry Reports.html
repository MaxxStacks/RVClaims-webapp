Frequently Asked Questions

Are reports free?
+
All reports are included free for active DS360 dealers. Non-customers can access summary versions and subscribe for full reports when they become available.
How often are reports published?
+
We publish major reports quarterly. Market updates and trend alerts are shared monthly via email to subscribers.
Is my dealership's data identifiable in reports?
+
No. All data is anonymized and aggregated. Individual dealership data is never shared or identifiable in any published report.
Can I request a custom report?
+
Enterprise plan dealers can request custom reports tailored to their region, manufacturer mix, or specific business questions. Contact your account manager or reach out via the contact page.
