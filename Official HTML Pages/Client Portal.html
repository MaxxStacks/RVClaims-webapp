White-Label Branding

Your Logo. Your Colors. Your Domain. Zero DS360 Branding.

Every element of the Client Portal is customizable from your dealer dashboard — upload your logo, set your brand colors, and configure a custom domain through CNAME. When your client logs in, they see your dealership's name and visual identity. DS360 and the operator layer are never visible.

Client Access

How a Client Gets Into the Portal

Access is controlled by the dealer — clients don't self-register. This keeps the portal exclusive to your verified customers.

1

Client Purchases

Client buys a unit from your dealership. The unit is added to DS360 by VIN.

2

Dealer Sends Invite

You generate an invite link from your dealer dashboard — tied to the client's VIN and unit record.

3

Client Activates

Client clicks the link, creates their account, and sees their unit dashboard with warranty info and all associated records.

4

Self-Service Begins

Client can track claims, submit issues, view documents, message the dealer, and access AI F&I — all from the portal.

Portal Features

What Your Client Sees When They Log In

Every feature below is available to the client through your branded portal — no phone calls, no emails, no waiting on hold.

Unit & Warranty Info

Complete unit details — VIN, specs, warranty coverage dates, and plan type — visible at a glance

Claim Tracking

Simplified claim status view — submitted, in review, approved, parts ordered, repair scheduled, complete

Issue Submission with Photos

Client photographs an issue, describes it, and submits through the portal — goes directly to the dealer and DS360

Parts Tracking

When parts are ordered for a claim, the client sees order status, shipping updates, and delivery ETAs in real time

Dealer Messaging

Direct messaging between client and dealer — threaded, timestamped, and stored in the platform. No lost emails.

Document Access

Warranty certificates, contracts, inspection reports, and invoices — stored in the client's file manager, downloadable anytime

AI F&I Presenter Link

Access to the personalized AI F&I session — browse and accept F&I products at their own pace, 24/7

Roadside Assistance

One-tap access to DS360 roadside services — basic or Titanium coverage, dispatched directly through the platform
