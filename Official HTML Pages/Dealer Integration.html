Common Integration Scenarios

Here are the most common ways dealers connect DS360 with their existing systems.

DMS Sync

Sync unit records, customer data, and service history between DS360 and your DMS. Eliminate double-entry and keep both systems current.

Accounting Export

Export claim invoices, payment records, and financial summaries to QuickBooks, Sage, Xero, or your preferred accounting platform.

Parts Catalog Sync

Connect parts catalog data to streamline parts ordering from approved FRC lines. Track parts across suppliers with automated status updates.

CRM Integration

Push customer activity, claim interactions, and service touchpoints to your CRM for a complete customer relationship view.

DS360 doesn't replace your existing systems — it connects to them. Your DMS, accounting software, and business tools keep working the way they always have, now with warranty and claims data flowing seamlessly between them.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which DMS platforms do you integrate with?
+
DS360 can integrate with any DMS that supports data export or has an API. We've worked with various platforms across the RV dealer landscape. Contact us to discuss your specific DMS.
Do I need a developer for integration?
+
Not for CSV import/export — that's point-and-click. For API integration, you'll need someone technical, but our team provides documentation, code examples, and support throughout the process.
Is data synced in real time?
+
API and webhook integrations support real-time data sync. CSV exports are on-demand or scheduled. The right approach depends on your workflow and data freshness requirements.
What about data security during integration?
+
All API communication uses HTTPS encryption. API keys are scoped to specific permissions. We support IP whitelisting and can implement additional security measures for enterprise integrations.
