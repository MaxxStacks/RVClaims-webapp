Titanium Coverage

Everything Your Client Needs When a Trip Goes Wrong

Titanium is not just towing. It is a complete trip protection package that covers the cascading costs of a breakdown far from home.

Extended Towing — 160KM+

Towing coverage far beyond the 50KM basic radius. Covers the full distance to the nearest qualified RV service facility or the client's preferred dealer — not just the first 50KM.

Roadside Repair

On-the-spot assistance — battery boost, flat change, fuel delivery, lockout service.

Trip Interruption

When a covered breakdown stops the trip, Titanium covers the cost of getting back on schedule — transportation and delay expenses.

Emergency Lodging

Hotel or motel coverage when the unit is undrivable and the client is stranded. Per-night allowance while the repair is completed.

Emergency Meals

Meal allowance for the client and passengers while stranded. Covers the cost of eating when the unit's kitchen is unavailable.

Vehicle Return Service

If the unit cannot be driven home after repair, Titanium covers the cost of returning the vehicle to the client's home or preferred location. The client does not need to make a second trip.
