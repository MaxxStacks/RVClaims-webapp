Did You Know?

DS360 charges flat fees — not percentages. A $250 marketplace commission on a $90,000 unit is 0.28%. Traditional auction houses charge 5-10% on the same deal. F&I products are wholesale — you set the retail price and keep the difference. DS360's pricing model is designed so the more you use it, the more you earn.

You purchase F&I products from DS360 at a wholesale price. You set your own retail price to the client. The difference between wholesale and retail is your margin. On top of that, DS360's cashback incentive program returns additional value based on the number of products you attach per unit — Tier 1 (1-2 products), Tier 2 (3-4), Tier 3 (5-6 maximum).
