Many warranty and protection providers exclude certain RV types from coverage — particularly toy haulers, truck campers, small trailers, and destination trailers. DS360 makes no such distinction. If it has a VIN and it is classified as a recreational vehicle, DS360 covers it with every service and every product in the platform.
