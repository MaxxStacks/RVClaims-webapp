Coverage

What Tire & Wheel Protection Covers

Road hazards that standard warranties and insurance policies exclude. These are the real-world scenarios your clients will face.

Blowouts

Tire failure from road debris, heat, or structural weakness — covered for repair or replacement

Punctures

Nails, screws, glass, metal debris — the most common road hazard for any vehicle

Sidewall Damage

Curb strikes, pothole impact, and sidewall cuts that make the tire unrepairable

Bent Wheels

Pothole impact, road debris, or curb damage that bends or cracks the wheel rim

Cracked Wheels

Impact damage that cracks the wheel — replacement required, not repairable

Curb Impact

Tight turns, campground posts, gas station curbs — RVs hit things car drivers never worry about
