Channel 3

Consignment Services — Revenue From Inventory You Don't Own

A client wants to sell their unit. Instead of turning them away, DS360 Consignment lets you list their unit in the dealer network and public auctions. You don't buy it, stock it, or finance it — you earn a finder's fee when it sells. For dealers, it is revenue from inventory that never touches your balance sheet. For clients, it is access to the entire DS360 buyer network for a flat listing fee.
