The Process

List to Close — Four Steps

Every transaction goes through DS360. You never deal directly with the other dealer until both sides have committed.

1

List or Search

List a unit for sale from your dealer portal, or search the network for a specific model, year, or price range.

2

Match

A buyer matches with your listing — or you find the unit you need. DS360 facilitates the introduction anonymously.

3

Escrow & Verify

Stripe holds a $500 escrow deposit. DS360 verifies the unit details, confirms both parties, and manages the documentation.

4

Close & Deliver

Both parties confirm. $250 commission applies. Escrow releases. Identities revealed. Unit is delivered.
