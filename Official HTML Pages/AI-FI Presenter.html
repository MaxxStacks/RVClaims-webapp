How It Works

One Session. Every Product. Zero Staff.

From the moment a client is credit-approved to the moment their product selections sync to your portal — fully automated.

1. Client Is Credit-Approved

The sale closes. Financing is confirmed. DS360 automatically sends the client a personalized link to their AI F&I session — via email, text, or through the Client Portal.

2. AI Avatar Greets by Name

The client opens the link. A live video avatar greets them by name, congratulates them on their purchase, and references their specific unit — make, model, year, and key features. It feels personal because it is.

3. Product Walkthrough

The AI walks through every available F&I product — Extended Warranty, GAP Insurance, Appearance Protection, Tire & Wheel, Roadside & Travel, Specialty Protection. Each product is explained in plain language with pricing relevant to their specific deal.

4. Real-Time Q&A

The client asks questions. The AI answers in real time — drawing from the Anthropic-powered knowledge base. "What does GAP cover?" "Do I need extended warranty if I have manufacturer coverage?" "How much is tire protection monthly?" Handled live.

5. Selections Sync to Portal

The client selects the products they want. Accepted products are confirmed and automatically synced to the DS360 operator portal for processing. No paperwork. No manual entry. The deal data is already there.

6. Automated Email Follow-Up

The AI sends and receives emails on behalf of your dealership — follow-up on pending product decisions, contract delivery, outstanding questions, and session summaries. If a client did not complete their session or left products undecided, the AI follows up automatically until the client responds or the window closes.
