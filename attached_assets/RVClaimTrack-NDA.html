Confidentiality and Non-Disclosure Agreement

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

Jurisdiction: Canada (PIPEDA Compliant)

ABSOLUTE CLIENT CONFIDENTIALITY GUARANTEE: Despite serving multiple RV dealerships across Canada, RV Claims Canada operates under the strictest non-disclosure protocols. Your sensitive business information, financial data, and operational details are never shared, discussed, or disclosed to any third party—including other clients or manufacturers.
1. Parties and Purpose

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between RV Claims Canada, a Canadian claims management company headquartered in Toronto, Ontario, with development operations in Montreal, Quebec ("RV Claims Canada"), and the undersigned RV dealership ("Client").

Purpose: To establish absolute confidentiality standards for all information exchanged during the provision of warranty claims processing, revenue optimization, and related services.

2. Definitions of Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes all business, commercial, financial, technical, and operational information disclosed by either party, including but not limited to:

  • Financial Data: Revenue figures, profit margins, cost structures, pricing strategies, and financial performance metrics
  • Customer Information: Customer databases, sales records, purchase histories, and personal customer data
  • Operational Details: Business processes, workflow procedures, inventory management systems, and operational metrics
  • Strategic Information: Business plans, marketing strategies, expansion plans, and competitive positioning
  • Manufacturer Relationships: Warranty terms, claim approval rates, manufacturer communications, and partnership details
  • Technology and Systems: Software systems, databases, technical processes, and proprietary methodologies
  • Personnel Information: Employee data, organizational structure, compensation details, and staffing strategies
3. RV Claims Canada's Confidentiality Obligations
3.1 Absolute Non-Disclosure

RV Claims Canada commits to the highest standards of confidentiality and agrees to:

  • Never disclose Client's confidential information to any third party, including other clients, competitors, or industry partners
  • Never discuss one client's business operations, performance, or strategies with any other client
  • Maintain strict information barriers between all client accounts
  • Use Client information solely for authorized service delivery purposes
  • Prevent any cross-contamination of client data or insights
3.2 Manufacturer Privacy Protection

Critical Commitment: RV Claims Canada guarantees that client and dealership information will NEVER be shared with manufacturers, including but not limited to:

  • Business Performance Data: Sales volumes, claim ratios, profitability metrics, or operational statistics will not be disclosed to any manufacturer
  • Strategic Information: Business plans, pricing strategies, inventory levels, or competitive positioning will remain strictly confidential
  • Client Lists and Relationships: Customer databases, dealership client information, and end-customer data will never be shared with manufacturers
  • Operational Insights: Internal processes, service challenges, or improvement areas identified during our engagement remain private
  • Financial Details: Revenue information, claim values, reimbursement amounts, or any financial data stays confidential

We work exclusively for you, the dealership. Your information is your competitive advantage, and we protect it accordingly.

3.3 Multi-Client Environment Protections

Recognizing that RV Claims Canada serves multiple dealerships who may compete in similar markets, we implement:

  • Separate Data Systems: Dedicated, isolated data storage and processing systems for each client
  • Team Segregation: Assignment of dedicated team members to prevent information crossover
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access ensuring team members only access relevant client information
  • Information Firewalls: Technical and procedural barriers preventing inadvertent information sharing
4. Security Measures

RV Claims Canada implements comprehensive security measures including:

  • Bank-Grade Encryption: All client data encrypted using industry-leading standards
  • Secure Storage: Data stored in secure, Canadian-based servers with redundant backups
  • Access Logging: Complete audit trails of all data access and system interactions
  • Employee Training: Regular confidentiality training for all team members
  • PIPEDA Compliance: Full compliance with Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act
5. Patent-Pending Technology Protection

RV Claims Canada's proprietary, patent-pending claims management technology is protected under this Agreement. Clients agree not to:

  • Reverse engineer or attempt to replicate our proprietary systems
  • Share technical specifications or system details with third parties
  • Use insights gained from our technology for competitive advantage against RV Claims Canada
6. Term and Enforcement
6.1 Duration

This Agreement remains in effect for the duration of the service relationship and continues indefinitely thereafter for all confidential information disclosed during the relationship.

6.2 Remedies

Both parties acknowledge that breach of this Agreement would cause irreparable harm. Remedies include injunctive relief, monetary damages, and legal costs.

7. Business Ethics Commitment
"Your trust is our foundation. Your secrets stay with us. Period."

This commitment reflects RV Claims Canada's core business ethics. We understand that trust is the foundation of successful dealership partnerships, and we protect that trust through unwavering confidentiality standards that exceed industry norms.
8. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by Canadian law and the laws of the Province of Ontario. Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Toronto, Ontario.
